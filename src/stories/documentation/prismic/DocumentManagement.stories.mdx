# Document Management

How to create, edit, and archive documents within Prismic.

## What are documents?

Documents represent pages, exhibitions, events, and more on the website. All
website content belongs to a document.

## Official Guides

The following guides from Prismic explain how to manage documents with in the
CMS.

- [How to create a new document in Prismic](https://user-guides.prismic.io/browse-documents-and-edit-content/editing-content/create-a-new-document)
- [How to publish content in Prismic](https://user-guides.prismic.io/browse-documents-and-edit-content/preview-publish-and-unpublish-documents/publish-your-content)
- [How to remove a document from your repository](https://user-guides.prismic.io/browse-documents-and-edit-content/preview-publish-and-unpublish-documents/unpublish-and-archive-a-document)
- [How to archive or delete a document from your Prismic repository](https://user-guides.prismic.io/browse-documents-and-edit-content/editing-content/archive-or-delete-a-document-from-your-repository)
- [Prismic keeps track of every change, allowing you to restore a previous version of a document](https://user-guides.prismic.io/history-revisions/versioning-and-restoring-a-document)
- [Use the Diff view to quickly view the changes that have been made to a document](https://user-guides.prismic.io/history-revisions/discover-what-changed-in-your-documents)
